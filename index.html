Profile Snapshot

I am an INSPIRE Faculty at the Department of Physics, Central University of Kashmir, working on the physics of relativistic jets and supermassive black holes using multiwavelength observations and theoretical modeling. My work combines Fermi-LAT, Swift, NuSTAR, NICER and AstroSat data with numerical and analytical solutions of the Fokker–Planck equation.
