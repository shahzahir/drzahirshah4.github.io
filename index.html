About Me

I am an INSPIRE Faculty at the Department of Physics, Central University of Kashmir. My research focuses on broadband spectral and temporal studies of blazars using multi-wavelength data, theoretical modeling, and time-domain analysis.

I obtained my Ph.D. from the University of Kashmir (2018) with a thesis titled Understanding the Spectral and Temporal Behaviour of High Energy Blazars.

Research Interests

  • Broadband spectral and temporal studies of blazars
  • Theoretical SED modeling and particle acceleration/cooling
  • Time-resolved spectroscopy and variability analysis
  • Statistical study of long-term light curves
