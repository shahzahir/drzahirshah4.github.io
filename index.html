I am an INSPIRE Faculty at the Department of Physics, Central University of Kashmir. I am Astrophysicist specialized in probing supermassive black hole environments through spectral and temporal signatures, with a particular focus on blazars and X-ray binary systems. My work combines broadband multi-wavelength observations from international telescopes like NASA’s Fermi-LAT, Swift XRT/UVOT, NuSTAR and India multiwavelength telescopes like AstroSat, with theoretical modeling and numerical solutions of Fokker Plank equation to uncover the physical mechanisms of relativistic jets, particle acceleration, and radiation processes. I have developed reproducible pipelines for spectral modeling, time-series diagnostics, and statistical inference, and applied them to individual flaring episodes as well as long-term population studies. Passionate about advancing high-impact research, mentoring students, and contributing to collaborative research and development and academic excellence.
